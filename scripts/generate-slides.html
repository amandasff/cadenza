Can you help me understand how to play the trill in bar 24 of Moonlight Sonata?
✦

Great question! The trill in bar 24 of the first movement should be played as a measured trill. Here's how to approach it:

1. Start on the main note (C#), not the upper auxiliary. In Classical period trills, starting on the main note is standard.

2. Finger grouping: Use fingers 2-3 for the trill. Keep your hand relaxed and use wrist rotation rather than finger strength.

3. Speed: Begin slowly — eighth notes at ♩=40, then gradually increase. The target is smooth 32nd notes at the marked Adagio tempo.
